Frequently asked questions










What happens with the donated money?


We use the money to sponser children's education and their shelter, food etc.







How many children can you sponser at once?


It is dependent on how much money we have at the moment where
around 1000$ is enough for 10 children per month excluding expenses such
as acomidities.







How do you guys allocate the allowance for each child?


Using an average of 1000$ per month half of it goes to food
and shelter and 400$ goes to need such as clothes, water bottles, shoes etc.
and the remaining 100$ can be used by the child however they desire under
supervision.
