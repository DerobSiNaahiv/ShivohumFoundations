Our NGO, which was founded by Shivohie Bhwaye, goes above and beyond to help the homeless. To help homeless people regain their independence, they offer education and vocational training in addition to shelter. By taking a comprehensive approach, they make sure that the people they assist not only find a safe place to live, but also acquire the abilities and information required to find steady work, thus ending the cycle of homelessness.
